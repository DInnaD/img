Solar Savings

In Hawaii, going solar can save you lots of money on your electric bill because burning oil to generate power is expensive! For example, oil-based electricity in Hawaii costs on average 40c/kWh, or about $300.00 per month for the average family of four*. By comparison, a Hawaii homeowner with solar on his roof can save up to 60%* on utility costs or about $180 per month or more than $2,100 per year.
